Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What We Collect

DOTHING does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All your tasks and data are stored locally on your device and synced via Apple's iCloud infrastructure if you have iCloud enabled.

2. iCloud Sync

If you use iCloud, your task data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

3. Trial & Purchase

Trial start date is stored in iCloud Key-Value Store solely to track your 7-day trial period across devices. No personal information is associated with this data.

4. Notifications

If you allow notifications, DOTHING schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

5. Analytics

DOTHING does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your behavior inside the app.

6. Children

DOTHING is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the App Store support link.
